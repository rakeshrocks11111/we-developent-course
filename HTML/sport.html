Judo

This is the best defending Game

judo, Japanese jūdō, system of unarmed combat, now primarily a sport. The rules of the sport of judo are complex. The objective is to cleanly throw, to pin, or to master the opponent, the latter being done by applying pressure to arm joints or to the neck to cause the opponent to yield.

IN JUDO WEIGHT CATEGIRIES

  • MEN : –60kg, –66kg, –73kg, –81kg, –90kg, -100kg, +100kg
  • WOMEN : -48kg, –52kg, –57kg, –63kg, –70kg, -78kg, +78kg

Rules of Judo

You cannot touch the opponent's face. Attacking the joints other than elbows is not allowed. Head dives are also not permitted. Some techniques like Kawazu gake and Kami basami are not allowed. Neither of the competitors is allowed to wear any hard metallic object during the competition.
